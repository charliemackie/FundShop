The Problem
Full cash refunds cost the retailer more than just a lost sale ...
1. Lost Sale to a Refund

2. Customer Acquisiton Cost (CAC): Once a customer recieves cash back, they're unlikely to make repeat purchases from the store. Now you need to double down on your CAC to replace the lost customer.



3. Sunk Shipping and Handling costs: the extra costs associated with returned items are now pure losses not even associated with a sale



4. Repeat Customers spend more: losing a loyal customer and replacing them with a new customer will decrease overall spend.

Our Solution
We'll provide instant refunds and let customers choose the proportion of their refund that is store credit vs. cash
